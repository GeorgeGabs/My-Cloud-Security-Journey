## Key Insights So Far

1. **Security is layers, not single solutions**
   - Cloud architecture + Linux + IAM + monitoring all work together
   - One layer failing doesn't mean total compromise

2. **Real attacks happen on your infrastructure**
   - Found actual brute-force attempts
   - Security isn't theoretical - it's practical necessity
   - Attackers don't target you personally, they scan broadly

3. **Understanding WHY beats memorizing HOW**
   - CIA Triad explains security decisions
   - Cloud models show why different tools fit different needs
   - Principles matter more than specific commands

4. **Small consistent progress compounds**
   - Week 1 seemed overwhelming
   - By Week 2: Confident and capable
   - Growth is real and measurable

5. **Cloud security is becoming standard**
   - Every company needs this knowledge
   - Job market is hungry for these skills
   - Learning now = competitive advantage
